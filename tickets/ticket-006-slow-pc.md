# Ticket #006 – Slow PC after login

**User:** Sales  
**Issue:** Laptop extremely slow for 5–10 minutes after boot.  
**Cause:** OneDrive syncing entire SharePoint library.  

**Resolution:**  
1. Limited OneDrive sync to selected folders  
2. Cleared OneDrive cache  
3. Restarted laptop  
4. Performance returned to normal  

**Prevention:** Provide standard folder template for OneDrive.
